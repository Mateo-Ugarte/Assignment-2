Steve Jobs


Founding Apple

Steve Jobs is one of the most important people in the history of computers. His first computer, the Apple I, was made in 1976. In 1977, he managed to obtain the financing, disturbation, and publicity for the company Apple Computer, and thus Apple was created. The same year he launched Apple Computer, he released the Apple II. The Apple II became a huge success, making Apple $139 million dollars.

Contributions

Steve Jobs helped found Apple, but he also changed electronics for ever. In addition to the Apple computers, he created iPods, iPads, and iPhones.
